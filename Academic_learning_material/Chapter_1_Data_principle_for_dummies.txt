Chapter 1 introduces data science as the discipline of gaining actionable insights from raw data. This process empowers individuals and organizations to make informed decisions and solve complex problems. The core of data science involves a systematic approach, encompassing data collection, cleaning, analysis, interpretation, and communication of findings. The field draws upon various disciplines, including statistics, computer science, and domain expertise. Ultimately, data science aims to unlock the hidden potential within data to drive innovation and efficiency across diverse industries.
